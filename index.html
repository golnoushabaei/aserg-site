Advanced Software Engineering Research Group

We study how to design, build, evaluate, and evolve reliable, maintainable, scalable, and responsible software systems.

About

ASERG brings together researchers and students working on modern software engineering challenges, focusing on impactful and industry-relevant research.

Research Themes

  • AI for Software Engineering
  • Software Architecture
  • Empirical Software Engineering
  • Software Quality and Testing
  • Developer Productivity
  • Maintenance and Evolution
