RMIT University

Advanced Software Engineering Research Group

ASERG studies how to design, build, evaluate, and evolve reliable, maintainable, scalable, secure, and responsible software systems.

Meet the team Research themes

Focus

Our work spans software engineering, AI for software engineering, empirical software engineering, software quality, software architecture, software testing, software engineering education, and professional practice.

About

The Advanced Software Engineering Research Group brings together academic staff, PhD researchers, and honours students working on contemporary software engineering challenges with strong real-world relevance.
